About This Project

NYC Playground Explorer is an interactive map-based application designed to help families, caregivers, and playground enthusiasts discover and explore New York City's diverse playground facilities. Our mission is to make playground information accessible, searchable, and easy to navigate for everyone.

Whether you're looking for an accessible playground for a child with special needs, searching for sensory-friendly environments, or simply want to find the nearest playground to your location, this tool provides comprehensive information to help you make informed decisions.

How to Use

üó∫Ô∏è Exploring the Map

  • Zoom and Pan: Use mouse wheel or touch gestures to zoom. Click and drag to move around the map.
  • Click Markers: Click any playground marker to see details and get directions.
  • Filter Results: Use the dropdown menus to filter by borough, accessibility, or sensory features.

üìç Finding Nearby Playgrounds

  1. Click the "üìç Near Me" button
  2. Either use your current location or enter an address
  3. Adjust the distance slider (walking or driving distances)

  5. Click "Show on Map" to visualize all nearby options

Click "üé≤ Pick Random" to discover a playground that matches your current filter settings. Great for finding new places to explore!

üèõÔ∏è Primary Data Source

This application uses playground facility data provided by the NYC Parks Department. The JSON is accessible at NYC Open Data. We are grateful to NYC Parks for making this valuable public data available to help families and communities access playground information.

  • Original Dataset: NYC Parks playground facilities database
  • Data Includes: Playground locations, accessibility status, sensory-friendly designations, and facility details
  • Usage: Educational and public information purposes
  • Updates: Data reflects NYC Parks Department records as of dataset creation date

Accessibility & Inclusion

This project is committed to making playground information accessible to all families, including those with children who have disabilities or special needs. We provide clear accessibility indicators and sensory-friendly playground identification to help families make informed decisions about playground visits.

If you notice any accessibility issues with this application or have suggestions for improvement, we encourage community feedback to help make this resource better for everyone.

Privacy & Data

NYC Playground Explorer respects your privacy:

  • Local Storage: All your favorites and settings are stored locally in your browser
  • No Tracking: We don't track your usage or collect personal data
  • Location Services: GPS location is only used when you explicitly request "Use My Location" and is not stored
  • Open Source: This project's code is transparent and available for review
